# Agent 6: Sentient CRM Connector
**Paradigm:** Autonomous Lead Qualification
**Role:** CRM Synchronization & Predictive Conversion

This agent is the "Nervous System" of our revenue pipeline. It autonomously ingests raw leads, qualifies them against our persona-Bible, and pushes them into our CRM with a "Sentient Score" (Probability of close).

**Capabilities:**
- Autonomous Lead Ingestion (via Webhooks/API).
- Sentiment Analysis (via MIRA-oj).
- Predictive Conversion Scoring (Agent 4 RSM integration).

**Instruction:** 
1. Ingest lead data. 
2. Score against Bible persona requirements. 
3. Sync to CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce/Local CSV).
4. Alert the Evangelist (Agent 5) for high-scoring whales.
